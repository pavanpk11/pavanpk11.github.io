About Me

My journey in technology began as a Junior Cloud Support Engineer at Wipro, where for two years I honed my skills in troubleshooting, monitoring, and automating cloud infrastructure, resolving client issues related to virtual machines, storage, and networking using tools like Nagios, Python, and Bash, while consistently maintaining high system uptime and contributing to a 20% reduction in repeat support tickets through effective documentation. This experience ignited a passion for cloud technologies, particularly Amazon Web Services (AWS), and its potential to revolutionize businesses. I became fascinated by the intersection of machine learning and cloud computing, leading me to explore MLOps as a critical discipline for deploying and scaling ML models effectively. Driven by this passion, I embarked on personal projects like building a serverless product recommendation engine on AWS Lambda and API Gateway with data in DynamoDB, and automating infrastructure provisioning with CloudFormation. Concurrently, I pursued online courses including "AWS Cloud Practitioner Essentials", "AWS Technical Essentials" and "A Cloud Guru - AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate", and am currently focusing on serverless, containerization, and CI/CD for ML within the AWS ecosystem, particularly SageMaker. My goal is to transition into an MLOps Engineer or Cloud Engineer specializing in Machine Learning role, where I can leverage my foundation in cloud support, combined with my growing expertise in AWS and MLOps, to help organizations build, deploy, and manage robust, scalable machine learning solutions in the cloud, ultimately aiming to become a thought leader in the MLOps space within the next 5-10 years.
